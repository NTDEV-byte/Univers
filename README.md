##How To

###Movements 

**Up Arrow or Z  to move up**
 
**Right Arrow or D to move right**

**Down Arrow or S to move down**

**Left Arrow or Q  to move left**


**P to Zoom in**

**M to Zoom out** 

###Tips

**Click multiple times a key to move faster in that direction **
